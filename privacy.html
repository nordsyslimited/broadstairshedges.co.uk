Privacy

Last updated July 2026

Broadstairs Hedges & Tree Services (trading name, operated by Richard Lim, NordSys Limited, Broadstairs CT10) takes data seriously. This page is the short version.

What I collect

  • If you contact me (email, phone, WhatsApp, form): your name, contact details, postcode, and whatever you tell me about the hedge. Used to quote and deliver the job.
  • If you visit the site: standard analytics — page views, approximate location, device type. Google Analytics 4 (GA4). No cookies for advertising or remarketing; I don't sell ads.

What I don't do

  • I don't share your information with third parties for marketing.
  • I don't add you to any mailing list without you asking.
  • I don't keep enquiry data longer than needed — typically 12 months after the job, then deleted.

Form submissions

The contact form is processed via Resend, which forwards the message to my hello@ inbox. Resend's privacy policy is at resend.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Analytics

Google Analytics records anonymised visit data. You can opt out via your browser's Do Not Track setting or by installing Google's GA4 opt-out browser add-on.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you can ask me to: confirm what data I hold about you, correct it, delete it, or stop processing it. Email hello@broadstairshedges.co.uk for any of those.

Cookies

The site sets a small number of cookies: GA4 for analytics. No advertising cookies.
