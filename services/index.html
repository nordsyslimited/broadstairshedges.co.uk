What we do

Four services. Clear pricing. No upselling.

Every quote includes the conservation-area paperwork if your address needs it, and every job leaves with all the arisings cleared. Specified for Canterbury chalk, the Stour-valley loam, and the Blean fringe.

Routine hedge cutting

Annual or twice-yearly cuts on privet, yew, beech, hornbeam, holly, laurel and Leylandii. Clean lines, on-site arisings cleared, no power tools after 6pm out of respect for neighbours.
